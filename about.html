Our Journey From a Simple Start to Serving All of Perth

Tailored Sweep Cleaning Services provides reliable, high quality cleaning for residential and commercial properties across Perth.

We started as a small, hands on operation and have grown steadily through consistent service and positive client referrals. Our focus has always been on doing the job properly and delivering dependable results.

Today, we service a wide range of homes and businesses across the city. While our workload has expanded, our approach remains the same. We pay attention to detail, communicate clearly, and treat every property with respect.



Our Commitment Cleaning Done Right

At Tailored Sweep Cleaning Services, quality and consistency guide the way we work. We take pride in providing thorough and reliable cleaning by using the right equipment, following clear processes, and maintaining high standards on every job.

We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide practical cleaning solutions that suit their space and schedule. Whether it is a one off clean or regular maintenance, each job is completed with the same level of care and professionalism.

No shortcuts, no unnecessary fuss, just dependable service you can trust, every time.
